BIG TASTY

The Big Tasty, not to be confused with the Big N' Tasty, is a burger available in parts of Europe, South America and the Middle East. It is made of a sesame seed bun, iceberg lettuce, tomatoes, Big Tasty sauce, cheese, bacon, and a beef patty or chicken patty.

BIG MAC

The Big Mac consists of two 1.6 oz (45.4 g) beef patties, "special sauce" (a variant of Thousand Island dressing), iceberg lettuce, American cheese, pickles, and onions, served in a three-part sesame seed bun.
